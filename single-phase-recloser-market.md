<p><p>The single-phase recloser market is categorized into two main types: single hydraulic systems and double hydraulic systems. Single hydraulic systems utilize a single fluid-powered mechanism to rapidly restore power after momentary faults, providing efficient fault clearing. In contrast, double hydraulic systems incorporate two hydraulic units, allowing for enhanced reliability and quicker response times under varying fault conditions. These systems are designed to improve grid resilience, reduce equipment damage, and minimize downtime in electrical distribution networks.</p></p>
