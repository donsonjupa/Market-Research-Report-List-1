<p><strong>The Titanium Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impression Die</li><li>Open Die</li><li>Rolled Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium forging market is categorized into three main types: Impression Die, Open Die, and Rolled Ring forging. Impression Die forging involves shaping titanium into complex forms using pre-shaped dies, offering high precision and tight tolerances. Open Die forging permits more extensive manipulation of the metal, allowing for larger components with less intricate shapes. Rolled Ring forging specializes in creating rings suitable for various applications, ensuring uniform thickness and material integrity. Each type serves distinct industrial needs within the titanium market.</p></p>
