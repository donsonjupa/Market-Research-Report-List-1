<p><ul><li>Material Extrusion Technology</li><li>Light Polymerization Technology</li><li>Other Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desktop 3D printers market can be categorized into three main types based on technology. Material extrusion technology, such as Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), uses thermoplastic filaments, heating them to create layers. Light polymerization technology includes techniques like Stereolithography (SLA) and Digital Light Processing (DLP), which utilize UV light to cure liquid resin into solid forms. Other technologies encompass methods like binder jetting and powder bed fusion, offering diverse material options and applications for various industries, from prototyping to hobbyist projects.</p></p>
