<p><p>The Industrial Thermoplastic Polyurethane Elastomer (TPU) market is categorized primarily into Polyester-Based, Polyether-Based, and Others. Polyester-Based TPUs offer high strength and abrasion resistance, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Polyether-Based TPUs provide excellent flexibility and hydrolytic stability, catering to applications in footwear and electronics.
