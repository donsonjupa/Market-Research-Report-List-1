<p><strong>The Automotive Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezo Buzzers</li><li>Magnetic Buzzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive buzzer market primarily comprises two types: piezo buzzers and magnetic buzzers. Piezo buzzers utilize piezoelectric materials to produce sound when an electric voltage is applied, offering benefits like compact size and high efficiency, making them ideal for applications such as alerts and notifications. Magnetic buzzers, on the other hand, rely on electromagnetic principles and typically provide a louder sound, making them suitable for more robust alert systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and user experience.</p></p>
