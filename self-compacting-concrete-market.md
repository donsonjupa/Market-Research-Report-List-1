<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Viscosity</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-Compacting Concrete (SCC) is categorized into three primary types based on its composition: powder-type, viscosity-type, and combination-type. </p><p>Powder-type SCC relies on a high volume of fine materials, enhancing flowability and stability. Viscosity-type SCC incorporates additives to improve resistance to segregation without excessive fluidity. Combination-type SCC merges the characteristics of both powder and viscosity types, ensuring optimal workability and performance in various construction applications. These types cater to specific project requirements, enhancing efficiency and structural integrity.</p></p>
