<p><ul><li>Spring Type</li><li>Lever Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure relief valve market is primarily categorized into two types: spring type and lever type. Spring type relief valves utilize a spring mechanism that opens at a predetermined pressure to release excess fluid, ensuring system safety. On the other hand, lever type valves operate using a lever and weight system, offering adjustable pressure settings for varied applications. Both types play crucial roles in preventing equipment failure by efficiently managing pressure levels in various industrial processes.</p></p>
