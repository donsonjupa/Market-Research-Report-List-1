<p><strong>The Electric Steering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric steering system market consists of three main types: C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering), P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering), and R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering). C-EPS integrates the electric motor into the steering column, offering enhanced vehicle feedback and efficiency. P-EPS places the motor on the pinion, allowing for a more compact design and better performance. R-EPS positions the motor on the steering rack, providing direct assistance and improved handling. Each type caters to different vehicle requirements and driver preferences.</p></p>
