<p><p>The bridge rectifier market is categorized into two main types: full-bridge rectifiers and half-bridge rectifiers. Full-bridge rectifiers convert AC to DC using four diodes, allowing for efficient power conversion and higher output voltage, making them ideal for applications in renewable energy and industrial equipment. Conversely, half-bridge rectifiers use two diodes and are cost-effective, suited for lower power applications such as consumer electronics and small appliances. These types cater to diverse requirements across various sectors and technological advancements.</p></p>
