<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Grade Single Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Single Crystal Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Single Crystal Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photovoltaic Grade Single Crystal Furnace market comprises primarily two types: Czochralski (CZ) and Floating Zone (FZ) single crystal furnaces. The CZ method involves pulling a single crystal from molten silicon, achieving high purity, suitable for solar cell applications. In contrast, the FZ technique uses zonal melting to eliminate impurities, producing high-quality crystals without contaminants. Both technologies are essential for manufacturing high-efficiency silicon solar cells, catering to the growing demand for renewable energy solutions in photovoltaic applications.</p></p>
