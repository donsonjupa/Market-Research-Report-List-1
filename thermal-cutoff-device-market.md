<p><strong>The Thermal Cutoff Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-use</li><li>Resettable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal cutoff device market is categorized into single-use and resettable devices. Single-use thermal cutoff devices function by permanently disconnecting electrical circuits when a predetermined temperature is exceeded, providing a one-time safety measure. In contrast, resettable thermal cutoff devices can be reactivated after a temperature trip, allowing for multiple uses. This versatility makes resettable devices suitable for applications requiring ongoing temperature control, while single-use devices are often preferred in scenarios demanding immediate and irreversible protection against overheating.</p></p>
