<p><p>The Active Harmonic Power Filter (APF) market is categorized primarily into Modular APF and Wall-mounted APF. Modular APFs consist of multiple interconnected units, providing flexibility and scalability for larger industrial applications, effectively managing harmonics and improving power quality. In contrast, Wall-mounted APFs are compact, designed for space-constrained environments, typically used in smaller commercial and residential settings. Both types serve to reduce harmonic distortion, enhance energy efficiency, and protect sensitive equipment from electrical disturbances, thus improving overall power system performance.</p></p>
