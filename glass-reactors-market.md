<p><ul><li>Thin Film Evaporators</li><li>Short Path Evaporators</li><li>Filter Reactors</li><li>Pressure Reactors & Rotary Evaporators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass reactors encompass various types designed for specific applications in chemical processes. Thin film evaporators facilitate efficient vaporization of solvents by creating a thin liquid film, enhancing heat transfer. Short path evaporators enable rapid distillation under reduced pressure, suitable for sensitive compounds. Filter reactors integrate filtration and reaction processes, ideal for solid-liquid separations. Pressure reactors allow for reactions under elevated pressure and temperature, enhancing yield. Rotary evaporators utilize rotational motion for gentle solvent evaporation, commonly used in labs for solvent removal.</p></p>
