<p><p>Metal Material Based Additive Manufacturing includes methods like Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses a high-powered laser to fuse fine metal powders layer by layer, creating complex geometries with high precision, making it ideal for aerospace and medical applications. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum environment to melt and solidify metal powder, offering advantages in producing large parts quickly. Other methods may include binder jetting and direct energy deposition, expanding the range of applications and materials.</p></p>
