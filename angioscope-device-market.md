<p><p>The Angioscope Device market comprises two primary types: Non-Occlusion Angioscopes and Occlusion Angioscopes. Non-Occlusion Angioscopes allow for visualization of blood vessels without obstructing blood flow, facilitating real-time observation during diagnostic and therapeutic procedures. In contrast, Occlusion Angioscopes temporarily block blood flow to enable clearer imaging and assessment of vascular conditions, often during interventions. Both types cater to specific clinical needs, enhancing the effectiveness of procedures in cardiology, vascular surgery, and related fields.</p></p>
