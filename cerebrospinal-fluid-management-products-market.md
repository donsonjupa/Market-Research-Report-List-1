<p><strong>The Cerebrospinal Fluid Management Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) management products market primarily includes CSF shunts and CSF drainage systems. CSF shunts are devices implanted to redirect excess fluid from the brain's ventricles to another area, preventing conditions like hydrocephalus. CSF drainage systems, on the other hand, are used to remove excess cerebrospinal fluid for diagnostic or therapeutic purposes, often utilizing external drainage devices. Both product types play crucial roles in managing disorders related to CSF accumulation and ensuring patient safety.</p></p>
