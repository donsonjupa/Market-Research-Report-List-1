<p><p>The preterm birth prevention and treatment market comprises several therapy types. Progesterone therapy helps maintain pregnancy by reducing uterine contractions. Corticosteroid therapy accelerates fetal lung maturity and decreases neonatal complications. Tocolytics therapy aims to delay labor by inhibiting uterine contractions. Antihypertensive therapy manages maternal hypertension, reducing risks for both mother and baby. Magnesium sulfate therapy is used to prevent seizures in preeclampsia and can protect the fetus's brain. Other therapies may include various supportive and alternative treatments to manage preterm risks.</p></p>
