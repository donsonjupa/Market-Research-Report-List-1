<p><ul><li>Straight Pancreatic Stents</li><li>Wedge Pancreatic Stents</li><li>Curved Pancreatic Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pancreatic stents market comprises three main types: straight, wedge, and curved stents. Straight pancreatic stents are designed for straightforward placement within the pancreatic duct to alleviate obstructions. Wedge pancreatic stents feature a tapered end, facilitating secure placement at strictures and minimizing migration risks. Curved pancreatic stents are contoured to match the anatomy of the pancreas, optimizing flow while reducing trauma to surrounding tissues. Each type addresses specific clinical needs, enhancing patient outcomes in managing pancreatic disorders.</p></p>
