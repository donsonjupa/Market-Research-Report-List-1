<p><p>The Marine Radio Detection and Ranging market primarily comprises two key radar types: X-Band and S-Band radars. X-Band radars operate in the 8 to 12 GHz frequency range, offering high-resolution imaging and effective for short-range applications like navigation and collision avoidance. In contrast, S-Band radars operate between 2 to 4 GHz, providing longer-range detection and better performance in adverse weather conditions, making them ideal for monitoring larger maritime areas and enhancing vessel tracking and safety operations.</p></p>
