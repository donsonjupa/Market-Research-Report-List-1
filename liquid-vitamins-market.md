<p><strong>The Liquid Vitamins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Soluble</li><li>Fat Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid vitamins market is categorized into water-soluble and fat-soluble vitamins. Water-soluble vitamins, like B and C, dissolve in water and are easily absorbed by the body, making them popular for rapid nutrient delivery. Fat-soluble vitamins, such as A, D, E, and K, require fat for absorption and are often formulated in oil-based liquids. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences, driving demand in health supplements, functional foods, and wellness products.</p></p>
