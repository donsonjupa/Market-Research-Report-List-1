<p><p>The Insulated Concrete Form (ICF) market primarily consists of three types: Expanded Polystyrene Foam (EPS), Polyurethane Foam (PUF), and others. Polyurethane Foam offers higher insulation value and is used in specialized applications where energy efficiency is critical.
