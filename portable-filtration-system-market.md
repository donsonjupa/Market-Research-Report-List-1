<p><p>The portable filtration system market includes various types of filtration methods tailored for different applications. Pressure filtration utilizes pressure to force liquids through a filter medium, effectively separating solids. Centrifugal filtration employs centrifugal force to accelerate the separation of particles from liquids, enhancing efficiency in solid-liquid separation. Vacuum filtration relies on a vacuum to draw liquids through a filter, removing unwanted solids. Each type offers unique advantages, making them suitable for diverse industrial processes and ensuring effective filtration solutions.</p></p>
