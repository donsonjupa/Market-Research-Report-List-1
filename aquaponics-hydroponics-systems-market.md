<p><p>Aquaponics and hydroponics systems encompass various market types including media-filled grow beds, nutrient film technique (NFT), and deep water culture (DWC). Media-filled grow beds use inert substrates to support plant roots, allowing for efficient nutrient absorption. NFT circulates a thin film of nutrient-rich water over plant roots, promoting growth while conserving water. DWC suspends plants in oxygenated nutrient solution, ensuring rapid growth. Other systems may include aeroponics and vertical farming, each offering unique advantages for sustainable food production.</p></p>
