<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Equipment</li><li>Cell Assembly Equipment</li><li>Post Processing Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid state battery manufacturing equipment market comprises three main types: pretreatment equipment, cell assembly equipment, and post-processing equipment. Pretreatment equipment prepares raw materials through processes like mixing and coating. Cell assembly equipment focuses on the actual construction of battery cells, involving layering and integration of components to form the battery structure. Post-processing equipment handles finishing touches, including packaging and system integration, ensuring the batteries meet quality standards and performance requirements before distribution.</p></p>
