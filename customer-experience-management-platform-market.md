<p><p>Customer Experience Management (CEM) platforms are categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise platforms are installed and managed locally within an organization's infrastructure, providing complete control and customization but requiring significant maintenance and upfront investment. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted remotely, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. They allow businesses to access features from anywhere, supporting real-time updates and collaboration, making them increasingly popular among organizations seeking efficient customer engagement strategies.</p></p>
