<p><p>The deflectable catheters market encompasses three primary types: unidirectional, bidirectional, and multidirectional deflectable catheters. Unidirectional deflectable catheters can bend in one direction, allowing targeted access to specific areas. Bidirectional deflectable catheters offer flexibility to navigate two directions, enhancing maneuverability during procedures. Multidirectional deflectable catheters provide the greatest versatility, capable of bending in multiple directions simultaneously, facilitating complex interventions. These categories cater to varying clinical needs, improving precision and effectiveness in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
