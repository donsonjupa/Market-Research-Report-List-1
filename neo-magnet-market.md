<p><p>Neo magnet market types include bonding and sintering processes. Bonding involves combining magnetic powder with resins or adhesives, producing flexible magnets ideal for consumer products and applications requiring intricate shapes. Sintering, on the other hand, involves compacting and heating magnetic powders to form solid magnets, delivering superior performance and durability. This process is commonly used for permanent magnets in industrial applications. Both methods cater to varying needs, contributing to the diverse applications of neo magnets in various industries.</p></p>
