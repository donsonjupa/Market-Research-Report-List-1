<p><p>Interactive digital kiosks are categorized into two primary types: floor-standing and wall-mounted. Floor-standing interactive kiosks are freestanding units designed for high-traffic areas, providing visibility and convenience for user interaction. They often feature touch screens and can be used for information dissemination, ticketing, and advertising. In contrast, wall-mounted interactive digital kiosks are affixed to vertical surfaces, saving floor space while delivering information efficiently. They are ideal for environments with limited space, such as retail stores and public transportation facilities.</p></p>
