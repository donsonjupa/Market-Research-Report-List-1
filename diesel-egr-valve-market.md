<p><p>The Diesel EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) Valve market is divided into two main types: low pressure and high pressure. Low-pressure EGR systems recycle exhaust gases back into the intake manifold, improving combustion efficiency and reducing emissions at lower speeds. Conversely, high-pressure EGR valves redirect exhaust gases from the turbocharger back into the intake, enhancing performance and emissions control at higher speeds. Both types play a crucial role in meeting stringent emission regulations and improving engine efficiency in diesel vehicles.</p></p>
