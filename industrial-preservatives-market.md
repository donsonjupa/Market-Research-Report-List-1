<p><p>The industrial preservatives market comprises various types, including fatty preservatives, inorganic salt preservatives, and biological preservatives. Fatty preservatives, derived from natural oils and fats, enhance product stability and shelf life. Inorganic salt preservatives, such as sodium benzoate and potassium sorbate, inhibit microbial growth and are widely used in food and beverages. Biological preservatives, often sourced from natural microorganisms, offer eco-friendly options that protect against spoilage while maintaining product quality. Each type targets specific applications and industries for optimal preservation.</p></p>
