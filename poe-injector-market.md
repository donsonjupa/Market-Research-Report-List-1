<p><p>The PoE injector market is primarily divided into two types: single-port and multi-port midspan PoE injectors. In contrast, multi-port midspan injectors can simultaneously power multiple devices, making them suitable for larger installations with numerous PoE-enabled devices. Both types facilitate the integration of power and data transmission over Ethernet cables, enhancing installation flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
