<p><p>Aerosol drug delivery devices are crucial for administering medications to manage respiratory conditions. Nebulizers transform liquid medication into mist for inhalation, ideal for severe cases. Metered-dose inhalers (MDIs) deliver precise medication doses with a propellant, suitable for various conditions. Spacers and valved holding chambers enhance MDI efficacy by reducing medication loss during inhalation. Dry-powder inhalers (DPIs) dispense medication in a powdered form, requiring patients to inhale forcefully, offering an alternative for those who find traditional inhalers challenging to use.</p></p>
