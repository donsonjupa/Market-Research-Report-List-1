<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) coating equipment market includes several key types. Sputtering deposition involves ejecting atoms from a target material onto a substrate, creating thin films. Evaporation deposition relies on the vaporization of a material, which then condenses on the substrate to form a coating. Other methods may incorporate techniques like laser ablation or molecular beam epitaxy. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of film quality, thickness control, and material compatibility, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
