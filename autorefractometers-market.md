<p><p>The autorefractometer market comprises two primary types: automatic keratometers and manual keratometers. Automatic keratometers utilize advanced technology to measure the curvature of the cornea quickly and precisely, enhancing patient comfort and diagnostic efficiency. They often integrate into comprehensive eye examination systems. In contrast, manual keratometers require the operator to align the device and make measurements manually, which can be time-consuming and less accurate but may be preferred in certain clinical settings for hands-on assessment. Both types play vital roles in vision care.</p></p>
