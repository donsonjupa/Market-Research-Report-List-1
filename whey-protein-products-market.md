<p><strong>The Whey Protein Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Products Concentrate</li><li>Whey Protein Products Isolate</li><li>Whey Protein Products Hydrolysate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whey protein products market comprises three primary types: concentrate, isolate, and hydrolysate. Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC) contains around 70-80% protein, retaining some fats and carbohydrates, making it a cost-effective option for general use. Whey Protein Isolate (WPI) has a higher protein content, usually over 90%, with minimal fats and lactose, catering to those seeking lean protein. Whey Protein Hydrolysate (WPH) is pre-digested for faster absorption, ideal for athletes recovering from intense workouts, though often at a higher price point.</p></p>
