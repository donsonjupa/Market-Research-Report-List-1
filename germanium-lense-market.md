<p><ul><li>Plano-Concave</li><li>Plano-Convex</li><li>Concave-Convex</li><li>Aspheric Germanium Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Germanium lens market features various types tailored for specific optical applications. Plano-concave lenses have a flat surface and a inward-curved surface, used to diverge light. Plano-convex lenses, with one flat surface and one outward-curved surface, focus light effectively. Concave-convex lenses combine both shapes, offering versatile focusing capabilities. Aspheric Germanium lenses are designed with a non-spherical surface to minimize optical aberrations, providing superior image quality and performance across infrared applications, crucial in telecommunications and thermal imaging.</p></p>
